What was the best part of your day? The worst? The funniest?

Most journal apps give you a blank screen and tell you to start writing. Not Mere. Mere asks three specific questions each day. The first two stay the same, while the third varies - It might ask, “What problems did you solve today?” or “What new thing did you learn today?”

Map

​Tag locations and see them appear on the map tab. 

Photos

Add photos to your daily entry, and view them later in the photos tab.

Stats

View information such as your average mood and how many entries, photos, and locations you’ve entered.

Practice mindfulness and boost self esteem.

Though we all know keeping a journal is great for your health, mindset, and communication skills, most of us don't have the time to write lengthy paragraphs. Mere is quick and easy, and takes less than 5 minutes of your day.
